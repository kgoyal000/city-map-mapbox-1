Add map marker

If the marker is not showing, please make sure that the address that you have selected is within the map scope.

The marker's placement is determined by the Google Maps address or coordinates in decimal format from Google Maps.

If you want to find the coordinates (latitude and longitude) of a specific location on Google Maps that doesn't have a formal address, you can use the following method:

  • Open the Google Maps website or app.
  • Navigate to the desired location by zooming and dragging the map.
  • Click/Press & hold on the specific location on the map.
  • A small information box will appear, showing the coordinates (latitude and longitude) of the selected location.

We are unable to add any additional marker' shapes or add more than 1 marker.
Our Triple map design allows for 3 maps with corresponding 3 markers.
